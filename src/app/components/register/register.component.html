THE AERIAL MAPPER

The Aerial Mapper is a web application running in the browser which allows users to upload and automatically stitch together drone footage to form a large composite map.

The Aerial Mapper web application allows users to upload bird’s-eye view video footage taken from drones of nature reserves. Uploaded footage is then split into multiple images and stitched together to form a large, high-quality map image. 

The Aerial Mapper overlays a grid onto the map as well as points of interest.
